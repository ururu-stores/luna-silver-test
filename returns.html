Return Policy

10-Day Returns

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 10 days of delivery for a full refund.

Conditions

  • Items must be in their original, unworn condition
  • The buyer is responsible for return shipping costs
  • Refunds are processed once the item is received and inspected

How to Start a Return

To initiate a return, please contact us with your order details. We will provide a return address and instructions.

Once we receive the returned item, your refund will be processed through Stripe and returned to your original payment method. Please allow 5–10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.

Questions

If you have any questions about a return, please contact us and we will be happy to help.
