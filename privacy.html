Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you make a purchase, Stripe (our payment processor) collects:

  • Name and shipping address
  • Email address
  • Payment information (card number, expiration, CVV)

We do not store your payment details on our servers. All payment data is handled directly by Stripe. See Stripe's privacy policy for details.

Analytics & Tracking

We use the Meta (Facebook) Pixel to understand how visitors interact with our site and to measure the effectiveness of advertising. The pixel may collect:

  • Pages viewed and actions taken (e.g. completing a purchase)
  • Browser type, device information, and IP address

This data is processed by Meta in accordance with Meta's privacy policy. You can opt out of Meta tracking through your Facebook ad settings or by using a browser extension that blocks tracking scripts.

How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill and ship your order
  • To send order confirmations and shipping updates (via Stripe)
  • To understand site traffic and improve our offerings

Third-Party Services

We share information only with the services needed to operate the store:

  • Stripe — payment processing and order receipts
  • Meta — analytics and ad measurement
  • Vercel — website hosting

We do not sell or rent your personal information to any other parties.

Data Retention

Order records are retained by Stripe for as long as needed to process transactions and comply with legal obligations. We do not maintain a separate customer database.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us.
